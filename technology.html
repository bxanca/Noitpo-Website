T E C H N O L O G Y


Year 11 presents an array of technology-focused subjects to explore. In Food Technology, students engage in both practical and theoretical work, combining two internals that involve planning and creating products with diverse components, while also comparing bread products. Fabric Technology encourages students to incorporate their identities and cultures into fashion through conceptual design and textile construction, addressing specific needs. Digital Technology offers flexibility with three internals, including options like database management, digital media development, computer system creation, and programming. These subjects equip students with valuable skills for diverse technological endeavours.

Digital Technology

There are 3 internals in total, Basic Processes (compulsory), and an options to choose one out of three internals that you may be interested in. Database, Media and Computer System. Then later on you start learning about programming, the different types of conventions and types. Digital technology is a very flexible subject and consume a lot of information that can be used in other subjects.
