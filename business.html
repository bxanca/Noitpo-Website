Economics

Exploring the economic state of our society and our country. With two internals and two externals. Focusing on government choice/policy and circular flow for the internals. For the externals the main focuses are on demand and market situations.

Achievement Standard 90987: Demonstrate understanding of a government choice where affected groups have different viewpoints. Resource reference: Economics 1.5 Resource title: Making an economic decision. Credits: 4

Achievement Standard Economics 90988: Demonstrate understanding of the interdependence of sectors of the New Zealand economy Resource Reference: Economics 1.6A Resource Title: Two Degrees of Separation Credits: 3

Accounting

In year 11 Accounting students learn a variety of different aspects that are important for early adulthood. Things like cash flows, Income statements and financial positions are crucial to understand in order to budget your finances correctly. Know where your money is going and how to improve your overall financial statues. Students benefit from taking accounting as it will benefit them in the future.
