S O C I A L - S C I E N C E S


In year 11, social sciences explore a variety of different skills that are used in todays society. Learning these skills are important as they help students develop into further careers. In History, you'll dive into the intriguing world of nuclear-free New Zealand, analyzing historical sources and writing essays on World War II while engaging in simulated interviews with black civil rights activists. Geography will provide insights into population dynamics, weather data collection, and geographic skills, enabling you to study environments shaped by natural events, conduct research, apply core concepts, and explore global-scale geographic topics.
