Dance

1.1 AS 90858 Compose Dance Sequences This is a three part choreography assessment in which you will be required to choreograph, either individually or as part of a group, three 45second dance sequences. You will be given a brief for each sequence which will give you direction when choreographing. You will need to draw on your knowledge of dance elements and choreographic devices in order to produce and develop effective movement material.


1.2 AS90002 Perform Dance Sequences This is a three part performance assessment in which you will be required to perform 3 45second dances. Each performance will be as part of either a duet, trio or group dance. You will need to demonstrate the ability to perform dance sequences with clarity and control drawing on a range of performance skills.


1.4 AS90860 Demonstrate understanding of the elements of dance This internal is a portfolio style assessment. You will be given a range of tasks both practical and written. Tasks will cover a range of dance elements and enable you to demonstrate your understanding through identifying, exploring and using the dance elements in different contexts.


1.5 AS90861 Demonstrate understanding of a dance performance This is an external exam, sat during the exam period at the end of the year. In class you will learn about and develop and understanding of a dance performance selected by your teacher. You will be required to answer questions about the movement, ideas and production technologies in the dance. Questions ask you to sketch, identify and describe different aspects of the studied dance performance.
