A R T S

Enjoy an exciting range of Year 11 subjects that appeal to your artistic interests. Dance allows you to explore the realm of expression and movement via portfolio work, choreography, and performance. In Art, you may express your creative vision via continuing art board projects that shows your artistic ability. Dive into the world of music, where you may experiment with group performances and composition, and take an end-of-year test to commemorate your musical journey. Meanwhile, Drama allows for self-expression through three internal assessments, one external assessment, and the exciting Class Play.



Dance

...

year 11 dance, it is mainly portfolio work. Two six credit Internals which is choreograph and performance. And an optional exam or another internal depending on what the class chooses. You explore a variety of different dance elements and techniques. Great subject to explore if you want to be put out of your comfort zone

1.1 AS 90858 Compose Dance Sequences
1.2 AS90002 Perform Dance Sequences
1.4 AS90860 Demonstrate understanding of the elements of dance
1.5 AS90861 Demonstrate understanding of a dance performance

Music

... Mainly portfolio work with an optional exam at the end of the year. In this subject you get to explore group performances and composition work. with a total of 14 credits overall. Music is a creative process/art form for students who love creating and sharing.

1. Group performance how to gel well with a group, cohesion and performence skills like stage presents
2. Composition good for song writing as well as understanding what goes into making a song like the production value and different recording softwares
3. Solo focuses on performance skills as well as intonation and how well you played the instrument
4. Exam focuses on classical theory side of music with plenty of Italian words, transposing and intervals i think
