L A N G U A G E S


In Year 11, students tackled three internal assessments in both French and Chinese, focusing on strengthening their language skills. In French, they delivered speeches as exchange students in France for the speaking assessment and created a French brochure and penpal emails for the writing assessment. In Chinese, they introduced themselves as exchange students in China for the speaking assessment, crafted a dialogue for a vlog about their Chinese school experiences, and wrote emails to students coming to New Zealand. These assessments aimed to enhance their communication abilities in both languages.




French

In French students had the chance to participate in three internal assessments. They completed the writing internal, the speaking internal, and will later in the year undertake the interaction internal. The main focus of their French studies this year has been on strengthening their language skills. In the speaking assessment, students delivered a speech in French, portraying themselves as exchange students in France. As for the writing internal, they crafted two pieces in French: a French brochure and an email to their imaginary penpals.

90882 Give a spoken presentation in French that communicates a personal response, 4 credits

90880 Interact using spoken French to communicate personal information, ideas and opinions in different situations, 5 credits

90882 Write a variety of text types in French on areas of most immediate relevance, 5 credits



Chinese

In Chinese, students also had the opportunity to engage in three internal assessments, mirroring their French course. They tackled the Speaking, Writing, and Interaction internals, with a primary emphasis on enhancing their communication structures in Chinese. In the speaking assessment, students introduced themselves as exchange students in China. For the writing internal, they composed a dialogue for a YouTube vlog, documenting their experience of Chinese schools. Additionally, they wrote emails addressed to students who would be experiencing school life in New Zealand.

90872 Write a variety of text types in Chinese on areas of most immediate relevance 5 credits

90870 Interact using spoken Chinese to communicate personal information, ideas and opinions in different situations 5 credits
